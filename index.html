About Me

Jomar Cabral Mata is from Brgy. Narvacan 1, Guimba, Nueva Ecija. He is a consistent honor student from daycare up to present, maintaining steady academic performance across all grade levels. He is a student leader involved in different school activities and responsibilities. He also works as a campus journalist, contributing written outputs for school publications and covering school-related events through writing. He takes part in school programs focused on environment, student participation, and campus activities. He handles academic requirements with discipline and follows structured study habits. He joins group tasks and school activities with focus on completion and accuracy. His experience in journalism supports his ability to write, observe details, and report information clearly. His involvement in school activities builds consistency in communication, organization, and responsibility in academic settings.
